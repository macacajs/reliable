Reliable

Testing management suite with continuous delivery support.

Try it Out →

Macaca Ecosystem

Seamlessly integrates with the Macaca toolchain ecosystem, seamlessly integrates with Mock, end-to-end testing, reporters, coverage, etc. to complete the pipeline closed loop.

Deployments Easy

Support multiple deployments within Docker, out-of-the-box service deployment experience to help you get your service done quickly.

CI/CD Runner

Supports mainstream R&D process software. Gitlab CI and Jenkins are integrated, and the reporting tool is scalable.

Tips

Reliable is suitable for working with existing R&D processes in the continuous integration phase, and can also be used independently.
